James' and Maurice's Cobb Salad

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

>Description

A Cobb salad is a main-dish American garden salad typically made with chopped salad greens (iceberg lettuce, watercress, endives and romaine lettuce), tomato, crisp bacon, boiled, grilled or roasted (but not fried) chicken breast, hard-boiled eggs, avocado, chives, Roquefort cheese, and red-wine vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • Grilled chicken
  • 3 dashes of crumbled bacon
  • Spring salad mix
  • Bleu cheese
  • Balsamic vinaigrette
  • Crushed black pepper

Directions

  1. Chop hard boiled eggs into 1 inch slices
  2. Cube grilled chicken into 2 inch chunks
  3. Sprinkle bacon across salad mix
  4. Add bleu cheese, eggs, chicken to mix
  5. Add balsamic vinaigrette and black pepper to taste
